SILVER CITY UPRISING PROGRAMS

Memberships is limited to African-Americans living in poverty. All SCU members will have access to the services based on the provisions of each program. There is no preferential treatment given to any one member. All members can sign up for programs as long as there are funds and space and they meet the provision set for each program. Approval for some programs may be based on their business plan, financial plan, financial circumstances and/or background checks for babysitting. Board of Directors and paid Staff members are not beneficiaries of Silver City Uprisingâ€™s programs.


TRAINING PROGRAM

  • Financial literacy training
    • Members will receive training on how to save money, building equity through investments and how to live within their means to decrease financial insecurities.
  • Computer training
    • Members will receive training on coding and many applications used for work, college and/or leisure
    • SCU will provide members with a database that enable them to self-learn.
  • Business literacy training
    • Members who fall into financial crisis seeking financial assistance of any kind will be required to take this training.

All trainings will be conducted by certified educators selected by SCU and based on their availability.
